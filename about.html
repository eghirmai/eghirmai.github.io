A bit about me

My name is Eva. I'm an economist based in Washington D.C. Originally from Seattle, I am passionate about improving policy decisions, which led me to work with organizations and governments around the world. I deeply care about our moral obligations to others, and I strive to incorporate this into my work.

In my free time, I like to explore my creative side. Photography is a hobby of mine and I use it to capture special moments during my travels. I also enjoy exploring design principles through maps and illustrations.
